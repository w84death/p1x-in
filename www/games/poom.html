PooM

PooM is a simple and fast platform game for up to four players. Challange Your friends! Age 4+, no violence. No download requied. Gamepads recommended.

You control one of the four character: blue, green, orange and pink. The goal is to collect as many block that match color of the character. There are many power-ups that affect the gameplay. Each player can put bomb that can destroy oponents and some items on maps (like wooden bridges).
