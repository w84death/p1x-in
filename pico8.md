Pico-8 is a [fantasy console](https://www.lexaloffle.com/pico-8.php?page=faq). But on our computers it's real and is super cool.

### Beast
- 128x128 16 colours
- 128 8x8 sprites
- 32k
- 4 channel chip blerps
